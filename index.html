Pai Nosso (Aramaico)

Avvon d-bish-maiya, nith-qaddash shmokh. Tih-te malkuthokh. Nih-we çiw-yanokh, ai-kanna d-bish-maiya ap b-arha. Haw-lan lakh-ma d-sun-qa-nan yoo-mana. Wash-buq lan kho-bain, ai-kanna d-ap khnan shwo-qan l-khaya-wain. Oo-la takh-lan l-nis-yoona, illa paç-çan min bisha. Mid-tul d-dee-lokh hai malkutha oo-khaila oo-tish-bookh-ta l-ah-lam al-min. Amen.
